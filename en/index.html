East West Dialogues

Siloism, Universalist Humanism and New Spirituality in the worldwide social context.

The initiative

From some debates made ​​in India between Siloists coming from various parts of the world, arose the idea of a gathering of people who adhere to the Universalist Humanism expressed through the Doctrine of Silo.

The Motivation

The initiative is pushed up by the need to continue to develop a wide interchange about the topics that are common to us, about our future direction and the importance to improve our activities and initiatives in the Asian Continent.

The promoters

The initiative is promoted by Siloists, members of The Message of Silo and Humanist Movement of Asia and Europe.

When

The gathering will take place on February 2015, form the 16th until the 22nd. The three peak days will be on 20th, 21st and 22nd.

”...Think about that... we have a disproportion between the total population and the total of the parks... Think about Asian population, all Asia and two parks, it is a disproportion...” (Silo, 17/8/2010)

Where

The meeting will be in Varkala, a coastal town overlooking the Arabian Sea, situated in the state of Kerala, south of India. It is about 50 kilometers north-west from Thiruvananthapuram (Trivandrum-International Airport)

The aspiration

We expect from this meeting to generate more clarity about our action in the immediate and future moment and to be the beginning of a dialogue open to further developments;
to promote and ecourage the exchange of experience and the contact between realities that translate the Siloist Doctrine in such different social and cultural contexts, regaining the common aspects.
Moreover, we hope that images of activities to be undertaken might arise, both in Europe and in Asia.

East West Dialogues — Varkala, India — February 16th - 22nd 2015

The implementation

During the 3 days of maximum concentration (20, 21 and February 22, 2015), there will be conferences, exchanges, seminars and workshops about the current situation of Siloism and the historical moment in which it is inserted, its past process and future possibilities.
In the previous days (16 to 19 February 2015) there will be initiatives of study and interchange that will be also useful to set the topics for the following days.

Organization

The development this project depends on the proposals and the initiative of the participants.
The organization of the event will be co-ordinated by a commission which creation is in progress. We are looking for interested people to join and take care of the functions necessary for the realization of the event.
At the present moment, the commission is working through monthly chat scheduled until the end of July 2014, within this date everyone who is interested can join the commission. Proposals for activities to be done during the gathering could develop until November 2014.
